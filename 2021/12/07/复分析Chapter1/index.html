「复分析」Chapter1：微积分

一、回顾

· 外微分形式（\(R^3\)）

· \(dx\)与\(dy\)外乘积记作\(\mathrm{d}x∧\mathrm{d}y\)，且有关系\(\mathrm{d} x∧\mathrm{d} y=-\mathrm{d}y∧\mathrm{d}x\)。
· 对外微分形式\(\omega\)定义微分算子\(\mathrm{d}\)，则有：

①\(\omega=f \rightarrow \mathrm{d}\omega=\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}dx+\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}dy+\frac{\partial f}{\partial z}dz\)

②\(\omega=P\mathrm{d} x+Q\mathrm{d} y+R\mathrm{d} z \rightarrow \mathrm{d}\omega=\sum\limits_{cyc}(\frac{\partial R}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial Q}{\partial z})\mathrm{d} y∧\mathrm{d} z\)

③\(\omega=\sum\limits_{cyc}A\mathrm{d} y\wedge \mathrm{d} z \rightarrow \mathrm{d}\omega=(\sum\limits_{cyc} \frac{\partial A}{\partial x})\mathrm{d} x\wedge \mathrm{d} y\wedge \mathrm{d} z\)

\(\begin{split}④\ &\omega =P\mathrm{d} x+Q\mathrm{d} y+R\mathrm{d} z\\&\Rightarrow \mathrm{d}\omega=\sum\limits_{cyc}(\frac{\partial R}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial Q}{\partial z})\mathrm{d} y∧\mathrm{d} z \end{split}\)
